Our Mission

Architecting the next-gen SaaS Cloud

We build cluster infrastructure tools that allow developers to provision, allocate subnets, and scale microservices instantly with absolute predictability.

Simplifying cluster operations for developers

Stackly was founded on a simple premise: provisioning scalable servers should not require complex configuration scripts. Our team designed a modular platform that packages containers, routing pools, database replica volumes, and load balancing nodes into unified point-and-click control grids.

Operating primarily from our Salem technical headquarters, we manage low-latency networks directly serving developers across the APAC and Southeast Asia regions.

Our Journey

From a single node to a Global Network

What started in a small workspace has evolved into a global movement. We realized early on that developers were spending too much time configuring servers instead of writing code. Stackly was born out of the necessity to abstract infrastructure complexities.

  • 2020: Launched first Beta cluster in Chennai.
  • 2022: Expanded to 15 global edge locations.
  • 2024: Achieved SOC2 Type II Certification.
  • 2026: Powering over 10,000+ developer ecosystems.
